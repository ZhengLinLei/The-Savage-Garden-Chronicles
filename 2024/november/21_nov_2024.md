# 21th of November 2024

Today is windy day, so I putted my plants inside the room to avoid any damage, but some of them like the venus flytrap and the sarracenias are in the balcony. Some of them are in dormancy condition so they are not in risk.

No sun today, so I decided to gave them some artificial light, I putted the full-spectrum light 50W for 4-5 hours.
